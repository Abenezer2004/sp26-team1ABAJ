About Sarah

I am a dedicated geriatric care nurse with over 8 years of experience providing compassionate in-home care for elderly patients. I specialize in Alzheimer's and dementia care, medication management, and mobility assistance.

My goal is to help seniors maintain their independence and quality of life in the comfort of their own homes. I believe in treating every patient with dignity and respect, and I work closely with families to ensure the best possible care.
